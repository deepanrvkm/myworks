Colt’s Drain Cleaning and Plumbing: About Us

Colt’s Drain Cleaning and Plumbing is a local, family owned company located in Asheville, NC. We provide a wide range of plumbing and drain cleaning services throughout Western North Carolina, 24/7.

A local, family business

A father of seven young children, Barry O’Brien and his wife, Deannia, wanted to build a plumbing company that would provide exceptional customer service. Colt’s Drain Cleaning and Plumbing is the culmination of all their hard work and determination to bring you a superior plumbing company that is second to none.

Top notch experience

Having worked in the plumbing industry since he was seventeen, Barry has over thirty years experience. Barry has unbeatable plumbing knowledge and has earned a “P1 Unlimited Plumbing License”- ensuring he’s qualified in any and all things plumbing.

Unparalleled knowledge

A business is only as good as its employees and Barry has made sure to staff only the best. Every lead plumber at Colt’s Drain Cleaning and Plumbing has between 15-25 years of plumbing experience. This ensures no matter the plumbing issue at hand, Colt’s Drain Cleaning and Plumbing will have the solution.
