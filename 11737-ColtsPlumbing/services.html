Asheville Water Heaters

A reliable water temperature is critical for your home. If you’re experiencing issues with your water temperature you may need water heater repair or replacement. Colt’s Drain Cleaning and Plumbing can maintain, repair or replace your current water heater.

You may want to have your water heater checked if you are experiencing:
  • Water temperature issues
  • Discoloration of water/visible rust in water
  • Water heater is leaking and/or making unusual sounds

Want to enjoy the benefits of a tankless water heater? We are certified in Tankless Water Heater Installation and Repairs.
