Event detail

Looking back at an inspiring CIAO event 2018!

Yearly, RightBrains organises the CIAO event together with organisations who embrace our mission: to grow and retain the number of women in digital technology. CIAO stands for creativity, intuition, agility and an open mind. Competencies that women can bring to the table. The purpose of the event is to inspire and connect digital technology professionals with young female talent. No boring lectures but inspiring stories given by industry leaders and professors from renowned universities in the Netherlands.

Digital talents and digital leaders

RightBrains and Computable invite organisations to nominate their digital talents and digital diversity leaders who have incorporated diversity as a mindset for success and innovation within their organisation. During this event we announce the winners. The purpose of the awards is to share inspiring role model stories and best practices.
